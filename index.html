Sometimes life can be overwhelming because we may experience hardships, stressors, life transitions and choices that impact our present. As your therapist, I am here to help you find your inner strengths, equip you with coping skills to begin the healing process. Together we will identify your sense of self for you to find that balance in relationships, career, work and your unique life transition.

WELCOME TO JENNIZ COUNSELING CENTER!
JenniZ Counseling is a safe place where we provide a supportive environment where you can freely explore your current concern for therapy. We will work together to create important and meaningful goals during therapy and move away from irrational thoughts, feelings or behaviors. My mission is to serve, help and empower you to remember your self-worth and gain the necessary tools to achieve your fullest potential to create mental health & wellness.
