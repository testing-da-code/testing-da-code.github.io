Telehealth

Telehealth is the use of telecommunication technologies to provide Mental Health Care via your mobile phone, computer or laptop with internet connection. This modality helps provide or support clinical care at a distance. Telehealth virtual visits within the comfort of your safe and private place.

Individual Counseling

Individual Counseling sessions for adults (18+) who live in Texas with weekly online sessions available.

Types of services I can support you with:
  • Life transitions (moving, migration, career, divorce)
  • Setting Healthy Boundaries
  • Relationship Issues
  • Stress Management and Burnout
  • Self-esteem and Confidence
  • Self-exploration and Personal Growth
  • Anxiety
  • Depression and Mood Disorders
  • Domestic Violence
  • Incest Recovery
  • Intimate Partner Violence
  • Trauma
