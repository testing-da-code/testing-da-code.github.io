About
Art Concrete Construction was created in 2023. The owner Angel Rodriguez has been working in concrete construction for 20+ years. At the moment we are a small family business with hopes of becoming a large company. It doesn't matter the size of the project, we always like to honor our name by making a work of art, through the cement.
Our clients are our number one priority and seeing them happy with our work, is the best!
We are dedicated to giving the best of ourselves to our clients. That is why when we complete each job, our work is of top quality.
