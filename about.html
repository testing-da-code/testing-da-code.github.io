Licensed Professional Counselor- Associate in the State of Texas MS, LPC-A
Hello! My name is Jennifer.

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor Associate for the State of Texas. I graduated from the University of North Texas at Dallas with a Master of Science in Clinical Mental Health Counseling. I hold a B.A in Psychology and minor in Criminal Justice. My professional background includes working with individuals with mental health illness and survivors of trauma in nonprofits and private practice clinical settings. I have extensive experience in multicultural competence working with refugees, domestic violence/Incest recovery survivors, and Individuals with mental and emotional health disorders. Currently, I am providing services to adults in both English and Spanish.

When I am not working, I enjoy traveling, collecting magnets, reading books, and having quality time with my familia.

My Approach

The treatment approach I use for therapy is an Integrative approach between Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy and Client Centered. My aim is to help you feel supported and heard in a non-judgmental environment. I want for both you and me to build a therapeutic collaborative approach as we explore and identify your thoughts, feelings and behaviors and move forward from challenging life situations to mental health and wellness. At the moment I am providing my services through Telehealth online in a safe, private, and comfortable space for you to unburden and be your authentic self.
